What is this?

This service allows users to search content across the Internet at various streaming services or to check if the content is available on Netflix for a particular country.

You can also check What's new on Netflix in your country.

Although I am not giving you this idea, I heard that some people use VPN to spoof that country's IP and stream directly on Netflix.

I don't know how they do that; I am a respected merchant and an excellent citizen of the Imperial City, the beloved capital of lovely Cyrodiil.

The Github repository is public and open source if anyone's interested.
